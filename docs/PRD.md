# **Academic Learning Platform**

## **Acellus Online Education System**

LAB University Christian Academy utilizes the Acellus Learning System to deliver a flexible, high-quality academic education that supports both traditional students and elite student-athletes.

The Acellus platform enables LAB U to provide a **structured, accredited academic curriculum while allowing student-athletes to maintain demanding training and competition schedules**.

---

# **Why Acellus is Used**

The Acellus learning system allows the academy to combine:

• rigorous academic standards  
 • flexible learning schedules  
 • personalized learning pace  
 • strong academic monitoring

This model supports students who are balancing:

* academic responsibilities

* athletic training

* travel for competition

---

# **How the Academic Model Works**

Students complete their **core academic coursework through Acellus during the morning academic block**.

Typical academic schedule:

**8:00 AM – 1:00 PM**

Subjects include:

• Mathematics  
 • Science  
 • English  
 • Social Studies  
 • STEM coursework

Students learn through:

* video-based instruction

* guided lessons

* assessments and quizzes

* progress tracking

---

# **Teacher and Tutor Support**

Although the curriculum is delivered through Acellus, LAB University Christian Academy provides **in-person academic support and supervision**.

Students receive help from:

• academic instructors  
 • tutors  
 • mentors  
 • learning advisors

Faculty monitor:

* lesson completion

* academic performance

* learning progress

* study habits

This ensures students remain **accountable and successful academically**.

---

# **Benefits for Student-Athletes**

Using Acellus allows LAB U to create a **balanced student-athlete schedule**.

Benefits include:

### **Flexibility**

Students can progress through coursework while maintaining athletic commitments.

### **Personalized Learning**

Students advance at their own pace when appropriate.

### **Accountability**

Progress tracking ensures academic standards remain high.

### **College Preparation**

Students graduate with a strong academic foundation while developing as athletes.

---

# **Academic \+ Athletic Daily Structure**

The LAB U schedule is designed to balance academics, athletics, and development.

**Morning (8:00 AM – 1:00 PM)**  
 Core academic coursework through Acellus.

**Midday Break (1:00 PM – 12:30 PM)**  
 Lunch and transition.

**Afternoon (12:30 PM – 3:00 PM)**  
 Electives and athletic development including:

* basketball training

* media and broadcasting

* coding

* photography

* podcast production

---

# **Academic Integrity & Standards**

LAB University Christian Academy maintains strong academic standards by combining:

• Acellus curriculum delivery  
 • faculty oversight  
 • structured study environment  
 • performance monitoring

This ensures students receive a **credible, college-preparatory academic experience while pursuing athletic development**.

---

# **Messaging Strategy for the Website**

• structured learning  
 • accountability

The goal is to show parents that LAB U offers **both elite athletics and serious academics**, not one at the expense of the other.

# **Facility Overview**

LAB University Christian Academy operates in partnership with the LABCITY Sports Training & Event Center, providing student-athletes access to professional-level training environments.

Students train in facilities designed to support:

• elite basketball development  
 • athletic performance training  
 • skill specialization  
 • leadership and discipline

The environment mirrors the structure and expectations of **Division-1 collegiate programs**.

---

# **Key Facilities**

## **Arena-Style Basketball Court**

The centerpiece of the LAB U training environment is a **professional arena-style basketball court** designed for high-level competition and development.

Features include:

• full regulation court  
 • game-ready environment  
 • spectator seating  
 • scoreboard and game atmosphere

This court serves as the primary location for:

• team practices  
 • scrimmages  
 • competitive games  
 • player development sessions

The environment helps athletes prepare for the intensity of **college-level competition**.

---

## **Dr. Dish Shooting Lab**

LAB University Christian Academy features a dedicated **shooting development room** equipped with Dr. Dish shooting machines.

Students can complete hundreds of game-repetition shots per session while receiving immediate feedback.

Training benefits include:

• shooting consistency  
 • form correction  
 • repetition-based skill development  
 • performance tracking

This system allows athletes to develop the shooting mechanics required at higher levels of competition.

---

## **Performance Training Center**

Student-athletes have access to a **performance training environment** focused on building strength, explosiveness, and injury prevention.

Training includes:

• strength training  
 • agility drills  
 • conditioning programs  
 • mobility and recovery work

Programs are structured to help athletes improve:

• speed  
 • vertical jump  
 • endurance  
 • physical durability

---

## **Skill Development Training Areas**

Additional training areas support individual and small-group development sessions.

Athletes participate in:

• ball handling development  
 • positional skill work  
 • defensive training  
 • game situation drills

These sessions allow athletes to refine their game in a focused training environment.

---

# **Academic Learning Spaces**

LAB University Christian Academy provides a structured academic learning environment where students complete coursework through the Acellus learning system.

The goal is to prepare athletes not only for success in high school competition but for opportunities at the **college level and beyond**.
