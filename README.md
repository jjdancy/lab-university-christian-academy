
# LAB University Christian Academy Website

Official website for **LAB University Christian Academy**, a Christ-centered K–12 private school located in Charlotte, North Carolina that blends academic excellence, elite basketball development, STEM education, and leadership training.

The goal of this website is to present LAB University Christian Academy as a **premier Christian academic and athletic institution**, comparable to a **top-tier private academy combined with a Division-1 collegiate athletic program**.

---

# Project Overview

This website is designed to:

• attract prospective students and families
• recruit serious student-athletes
• showcase academic excellence and athletic development
• build trust and authority for the academy
• drive admissions inquiries and tour bookings

The site emphasizes **faith, discipline, preparation, and elite development**.

# Academic Model

LAB University Christian Academy utilizes the Acellus Learning System to deliver flexible, structured academic instruction that supports student-athletes.

Students complete their core coursework during the morning academic block while receiving guidance and supervision from instructors and tutors.

Typical academic schedule:

Morning (8:00 AM – 12:00 PM)
Core academic courses through Acellus.

Afternoon (12:30 PM – 3:00 PM)
Electives and athletic development.

Core subjects include:

• Mathematics
• Science
• English
• Social Studies
• STEM-focused coursework

---
